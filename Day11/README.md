# Day 11: Mastery of Stacks and Queues

**LeetCode Visualization Link:** [Bracket Balancer Buddy](https://bracket-balancers-buddy.lovable.app)

Welcome to Day 11! Today we take a deep dive into the practical applications and implementations of two fundamental linear data structures: **Stacks** (LIFO) and **Queues** (FIFO).


#resources: https://www.wscubetech.com/resources/dsa/data-structure-types
## 🎯 Today's Topics

1. **The LIFO & FIFO Principles**
   - Deep understanding of Last-In, First-Out and First-In, First-Out mechanics.
2. **Core Operations & Variations**
   - Push/Pop vs Enqueue/Dequeue.
   - Deques (Double Ended Queues), Priority Queues, Circular Queues, and Min Stacks.
3. **Implementation in Python**
   - Comparing built-in `list`, `collections.deque`, and thread-safe alternatives (`queue.LifoQueue`, `queue.Queue`).
4. **Powerful Applications**
   - Validating Parentheses, Monotonic Stacks, Task Scheduling, and Print Spoolers.
