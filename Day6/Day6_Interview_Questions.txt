Q1: What is the time complexity of a standard `for` loop?
A: O(N)

Q2: What is the time complexity of two separate (non-nested) `for` loops?
A: O(N) + O(N) = O(2N) -> simplifies to O(N)

Q3: What is the time complexity of a nested loop (loop inside a loop)?
A: O(N) * O(N) = O(N^2)

Q4: What is the time complexity of popping the last element from a Python list?
A: O(1)

Q5: What is the time complexity of inserting an element at the beginning of a Python list?
A: O(N), because all subsequent elements must be shifted over in memory.
