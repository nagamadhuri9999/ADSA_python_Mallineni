## 3. Merge Sort
- Concept: Split array in halves, sort, and merge.
- Implementation of the `merge()` helper.
- Time Complexity (O(n log n)) and Space Complexity (O(n)).

## 4. Quick Sort
- Concept: Pick a pivot, partition the array, and sort recursively.
- Implementation of the `partition()` helper.
- Time Complexity (Best/Avg: O(n log n), Worst: O(n^2)).
- Space Complexity (O(log n) for call stack).
